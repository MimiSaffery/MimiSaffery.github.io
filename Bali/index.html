Destinations in Bali

Seminyak

Seminyak is a popular beach ressort on the southern coast of Bali. Seminyak is the most popular destination for western tourists because of its lively bars, beautiful restaurants and instagramable locations. Famous locations in Bali such as Potato Head, Motel Mexicola and Mrs Sippy Bali.

Ubud

Ubud is a town in Bali surrounded by terraced rice paddies and beautiful hindu temples. Hence, Ubud is known as the cultural capital of Bali. Ubud is also well known for its magnificent art galleries, shopping, waterfalls, Sacred Monkey Forest.

Nusa Penida

Nusa Penida is an island located south east of Bali. Nusa Penida is a small and quiet island with extrandoniary beaches Kelingking Beach, Crystal Bay Beach and Diamond Beach. Nusa Penida is also a great location to go diving or snorkelling with manta rays at manta point.

Gili Islands

Gili Islands are three very small islands - Gili Trawangan, Gili Meno and Gili Air - located north east of Bali. Gili Trawagan is the most popular island among backpackers having many hostels, bars and nightclubs. However, further up the coast there is more luxury villas, homestays and hotels for coupleas and family. Gili T has beautiful restaurants, incredible diving and snorkelling experiences and tours despite being known as the "party island". Gili Memo is more favoured by couples seeking a 'romantic escape'. Gili Memo is a peaceful island full of white sand beaches and crystal blue oceans, however, the number of restaurants is limited. Gili Air is a mixture of both islands having many facilities and romantic hotspots. Gili Air is also a very great spot for Yoga.
